Who Am I?

Hi! Thank you for taking the time to look at my page.

I'm Hollis Kuang, a first-generation college graduate. I currently work as a Management Consultant based out of San Francsico. When I'm not thinking about the bottom line, I am most likely progamming. I also exercise . . . sometimes, every now and then, when I get the chance.

I think a lot about the beauty and complexity of what we are able to derive out of simple binary. That is why I code.

From Humble Beginnings

My first programming language was , in a course where we used DrJava, an IDE which was first released around when I was born. Unfortunately, the students who now take the course are forced to use IntelliJ. Throughout the semester, I was able to uncover the optimal solution to the elusive Traveling Salesman Problem but not what public static void main(String[] args) meant.

Eventually, I learned about dynamically typed languages like Javascript and thought that I was saved. But nowaways, I find myself using the strongly-typed versions of weakly-typed languages. It's funny how things work out. Thank you Microsoft!

On that note, I love the Open Source Communnity that you can find in progamming. It's what keeps me coding. It's a culture of collaboration and selflessness that you will rarely find anywhere else.                 And it is beautiful in a way, how interconnected the world wide web is because of these very principles. It inspires me and makes me hope that one day, I can also contribute back to this community which I have gained so much knowledge from. (I see a lot of isEven npm packages but not a lot of isOdd packages)
